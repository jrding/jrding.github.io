What is Triathlon?

Triathlon is a sport that involves swimming, biking, and running. Athletes begin the race with an open water swim, transition onto
a bike ride, and then finish with a run. The sprint distance triathlon consists of a 750 meter swim, 20k bike, and a 5k run.

Paratriathlon has a similar style to triathlon, but the athletes that compete in paratriathlon have some kind of physical disability or a visual impairment. Blind or visually impaired athletes race with the assistance of a sighted guide. Other athletes use various types of adaptive equipment.
